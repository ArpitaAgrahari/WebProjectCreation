Our Global Campus

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university located in Chaheru, Phagwara, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2005 by Lovely International Trust, under The Lovely Professional University Act, 2005 (Punjab Act 25 of 2005)and started operation in 2006.The university campus is spread over 600 acres in Chaheru Village on the Jalandhar-Delhi GT Road

Our Facilities

Lovely Professional University is one of the World's largest Universities in terms of providing lodging facilities to its students. It has the capacity to accommodate approx 25,000 of its students on its campus.Apart from this, students are also permitted to ply their private two-wheelers or four-wheelers, for which a sufficient parking area is available near every academic block.

World Class Library

LPU academic community is well catered by all departmental libraries.There are more than 24+ lakh books and e-books on all subject fields.Library system subscribes number of journals, magazines and newspapers.
